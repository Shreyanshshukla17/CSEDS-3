United Nations Faces Deep Budget Cuts: 1 in 5 Jobs on the Line - Who's Protected, Who's Not?

The United Nations is confronting a significant funding crisis, leading to reductions of over 15% in the regular budget and nearly 19% in staff positions for 2026. Secretary-General Antonio Guterres emphasized that these cuts have been strategically planned to protect the most vulnerable nations, including least developed and landlocked countries.
